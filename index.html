🗺️ What is CoffIE?
CoffIE is Ireland's live coffee map — helping you find great cafés near you, check prices, read reviews, and discover hidden gems across the island. Built by a coffee lover, for coffee lovers.
💰 Prices
All prices are crowd-sourced — submitted by real customers like you. Spot a wrong price? Submit the correct one and help keep CoffIE accurate for everyone.
🏠 Are you a café owner?
Claim your listing to update your prices, add photos, and get featured on the map. Owner dashboards coming soon.
